Conventional Loans

Traditional mortgage options with competitive rates and flexible terms for qualified buyers.

  • Competitive interest rates
  • Fixed or adjustable rate options
  • 15, 20, or 30-year terms
  • Lower down payment options available

FHA Loans

Government-backed loans perfect for first-time homebuyers with lower down payment requirements.

  • As low as 3.5% down payment
  • More flexible credit requirements
  • Lower closing costs
  • Ideal for first-time buyers

VA Loans

Exclusive benefits for military service members, veterans, and eligible spouses.

  • $0 down payment required
  • No private mortgage insurance
  • Competitive interest rates
  • Limited closing costs

Refinancing

Lower your rate, reduce monthly payments, or tap into your home's equity.

  • Rate and term refinancing
  • Cash-out refinancing
  • Streamline options available
  • Potentially lower payments

Your Path to Homeownership

A simple, straightforward process from application to closing

1

Submit Your Application

Start by completing your online application at kwlends.com/loan-officer/mfields. The process is quick, secure, and takes just minutes. You'll provide basic information about your income, employment, assets, and the property you're interested in purchasing.

2

Get Pre-Approved

After reviewing your application, I'll work with you to secure a pre-approval letter. This shows sellers you're a serious buyer and gives you confidence in your budget. I'll guide you through document collection and answer any questions you have.

3

Find Your Perfect Home

With your pre-approval in hand, you're ready to shop with confidence. I'll be available throughout your home search to discuss loan options, answer questions, and adjust your pre-approval if needed.

4

Finalize Your Loan

Once you've found your dream home, I'll lock in your rate and guide you through the underwriting process. I'll keep you informed every step of the way and ensure a smooth path to closing.

5

Close and Celebrate

On closing day, you'll sign the final paperwork and receive your keys! I'll be there to answer any last-minute questions and ensure you're completely comfortable with your new mortgage. Welcome home!

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common mortgage questions

Down payment requirements vary by loan type. Conventional loans can be as low as 3%, FHA loans 3.5%, VA and USDA loans offer $0 down options. I'll help you find the option that works best for your situation.

Credit requirements vary by loan program. While conventional loans typically require 620+, FHA loans may accept scores as low as 580. Don't let credit concerns stop you from applying - let's discuss your options.

From application to closing typically takes 30-45 days. Pre-approval can often be completed within 24-48 hours. I'll work efficiently to keep your timeline on track.

You'll need proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns), bank statements, employment verification, and ID. I'll provide a complete checklist and help you gather everything needed.

Absolutely! Getting pre-approved is highly recommended. It shows sellers you're a serious buyer and helps you understand your budget before falling in love with a home.

Refinancing can make sense if rates have dropped, you want to shorten your loan term, or need to access your home's equity. I can run the numbers to see if refinancing benefits you.
