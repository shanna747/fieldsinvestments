About Margie Fields

Building communities and creating investment opportunities

Passionate About Transforming Properties

I specialize in identifying properties with untapped potential and transforming them into valuable assets that serve families.

My journey in real estate began with a simple belief: every property has a story, and every investment has the power to create positive change. Whether it's revitalizing a historic home, helping a family find their forever home, or partnering with investors to aquire property, I approach each project with dedication, integrity, and a commitment to excellence.

I've successfully completed fix and flip projects, provided mortgage lending and established strong partnerships with private lenders and investment collaborators. My approach combines strategic market analysis, hands-on project management, and transparent communication to ensure every investment delivers results.

Beyond the numbers, I'm passionate about revitalizing communities and creating homes where families can thrive. Each project is an opportunity to enhance neighborhoods while generating solid returns for my partners, clients and investors.
